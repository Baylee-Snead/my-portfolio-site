About Me...

I’m a Web Designer based in Brevard, NC who’s passionate about creating beautiful design solutions that enhance users’ experience and meet their needs. In addition to designing websites, I also design banners, flyers, and landing pages.

I enjoy hand coding sites and take a mobile first approach so that sites I build look great across all mobile, tablet, and desktop screens. My favorite thing about what I do is getting to connect with people and creating something beautiful for them.

When I’m not designing or coding, I’m probably out riding my mountain bike, shooting some film with my drone, or travelling.
